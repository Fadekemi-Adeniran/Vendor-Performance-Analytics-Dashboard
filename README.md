# Vendor-Performance-Analytics-Dashboard
Vendor Performance Analytics Dashboard | Power BI solution for evaluating supplier efficiency, procurement spend, delivery performance, and purchasing trends


## Objective
To analyze vendor performance and procurement activities, evaluate supplier efficiency, identify top-performing vendors and products, and deliver actionable insights that support strategic sourcing and procurement decision-making

## Tools & Techniques
- **Tools Used:** Power BI , DAX, Microsoft Excel, SQL, Power Query
- Developed performance metrics to evaluate supplier efficiency, purchase volumes, delivery performance, and procurement trends.
- Built a procurement analytics model integrating Vendor, Product, Purchase Orders, Date, and Location dimensions.
- Leveraged Power Query to transform and prepare purchasing data for accurate reporting and analysis..
- Designed executive-level dashboards to support supplier evaluation, spend analysis, and procurement optimization.
- Applied data visualization techniques to identify high-performing vendors, purchasing patterns, and sourcing opportunities.

## Key Insights
From the dashboard:
- Procurement spend exceeded **55 billion**, demonstrating significant purchasing activity across vendors and product categories.
- Top-performing vendors achieved delivery efficiency rates above **99%**, indicating a highly reliable supplier network.
- CISNEROS, Muskaan, EATON, Montgomery, and FROST, Maude ranked among the highest-performing vendors based on delivery efficiency.
- Procurement activity remained relatively stable throughout the year, with monthly purchase volumes consistently exceeding **4.9 billion**.
- Vendor performance analysis revealed measurable differences in supplier efficiency, supporting data-driven vendor selection and contract management.
- A small group of vendors contributed disproportionately to procurement performance, highlighting strategic supplier relationships

## Business Impact
- Enabled data-driven supplier evaluation and performance monitoring.
- Identified high-performing vendors to support strategic sourcing decisions.
- Improved visibility into procurement spend and purchasing trends.
- Supported supplier risk management through delivery efficiency tracking.
- Enhanced procurement planning through interactive vendor and product analysis.
